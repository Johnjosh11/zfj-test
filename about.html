About us

Zelous for Jesus is a welcoming church family that exists to worship Jesus, make disciples, serve people and carry hope into every generation.

Who we are

Passionate about Jesus and people

We are a growing community of believers from different backgrounds, ages and cultures. We believe church should feel like family and every person should find a place to belong.

Our gatherings include worship, Bible teaching, prayer, fellowship and opportunities to serve.

Our values

Jesus first: Everything we do is centred on Him.

Prayer: We believe God hears and answers prayer.

Community: We grow better together.

Mission: We serve our neighbourhood and beyond.
