Our Zealous Story

Zealous for Jesus isn't just a church; it's a spiritual circus! Founded by the ever-so-slightly eccentric Pastor Bartholomew, we're on a mission to spread love, laughter, and maybe a little bit of holy chaos. We believe in Jesus, but we also believe in having a good time while doing it.

Our vision is simple: to create a community where everyone feels welcome, even if they accidentally wear socks with sandals. We value authenticity, compassion, and the occasional spontaneous dance-off. Don't be surprised if you find yourself breaking into song during the sermon – it happens more often than you think.

So, if you're looking for a church that's a little bit different, a little bit quirky, and a whole lot of fun, then Zealous for Jesus is the place for you. Come join us as we stumble, laugh, and occasionally get things right on this wild and wonderful journey of faith. Warning: May cause excessive smiling and uncontrollable urges to hug strangers.

Fuel the Fire!

Want to help us spread the Gospel like wildfire? Your generous donations keep our ministries burning bright. Every penny counts (except for those pesky Canadian ones, nobody likes those).

Seriously though, your support allows us to reach more souls and make a real difference in the world. Think of it as an investment in eternal happiness (with a guaranteed return!).

You can donate online, by mail, or even in person (we accept cash, checks, and even the occasional goat). Thank you for your unwavering support! (And please, no more goats.)

New Here? Welcome!

So, you're thinking about joining the Zealous for Jesus family? Excellent choice! We're a little bit quirky, a little bit loud, and a whole lot of love. Prepare to be welcomed with open arms (and maybe a slightly awkward hug).

We know walking into a new church can be intimidating, but don't worry, we don't bite (unless you're made of chocolate). Come as you are, and get ready to experience the joy of fellowship with a community that's truly passionate about Jesus. We can't wait to meet you!
